People Make the Difference

Since 1955, we have been creating living spaces with character and solid value with commitment and enthusiasm. Our employees carry this attitude and joy within them. The environment for mutual interaction and the development of our employees is shaped by the following values:

  • Trust
    We develop and maintain long-term partnerships that are responsible, fair and binding. This creates space for the preservation and development of different needs.
  • Ambitions
    We are characterized by positive collaboration, mutual support and respect, joy in beauty and a healthy dose of humor.
  • Living Spaces
    We offer exciting areas of responsibility with interesting creative opportunities. Short decision-making paths allow for quick problem-solving. Our office spaces and infrastructure reflect our professional standards.
  • Commitment
    We offer attractive employment conditions, flexible work models and opportunities to actively contribute to the further development of Immofonds Asset Management AG.

Open Positions

A team is more than just the sum of its parts. We are looking for colleagues who want to contribute proactively, think along and develop the company further with professional competence and joy. We look forward to getting to know you.
