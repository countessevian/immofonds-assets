Competencies

Business Model

As fund management and asset manager operating independently of large financial service providers, we launch and manage products in the field of collective investments. As specialized real estate asset managers of Swiss direct real estate investments, we offer institutional and private investors the opportunity to invest indirectly in the Swiss real estate market.

Investors benefit from the highest expertise, independence and agility, achieving stable, recurring returns.

Immofonds Asset Management AG invests with two funds primarily in recession-resistant residential properties throughout Switzerland. The property portfolio in long-term good locations and with high construction quality is continuously renovated and renewed and thus further developed. Capital requirements for moderate growth are secured through issuances.

The sustainable use of available resources is of great importance to us and flows into the principles of corporate management and portfolio planning and development. We are committed to creating living spaces with character, conducting business responsibly, protecting the environment and communicating transparently.

More about our sustainability strategy can be found here.

Value Creation

All activities of Immofonds Asset Management AG focus on investment management (acquisitions, divestitures, development of and investments in existing properties) as well as portfolio and asset management of the real estate assets it manages. Through active asset management, potentials are to be realized and the long-term earning power of the properties managed is to be maintained and strengthened.

Throughout the entire life cycle of the properties, we look for sustainable development opportunities to sensibly increase the value of the portfolio. In addition, we systematically identify investment perspectives that enable long-term growth of the assets managed.

Strengths

Our passion for real estate is our strength.

Independence
Since 1955, we have been operating independently and searching for Swiss real estate with long-term prospects, free from conflicts of interest.

Growth & Performance
On a stable growth path, we currently have around CHF 2.0 billion in assets under management and a development pipeline of CHF 700 million, with which attractive and stable returns are generated with appropriate risk.

Industry Expertise
Comprehensive know-how of the Swiss real estate market built up over 60 years makes us proven real estate experts who operate in the market with a clearly structured, transparent investment process.

Team
At Immofonds Asset Management, highly trained people work with passion and motivation for the Swiss real estate market and their projects.

ESG
Responsible business management, conscious use of natural resources and increasing use of renewable energies are clear objectives and create social added value.
